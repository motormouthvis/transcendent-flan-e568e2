3309 N Indian River Drive, St Lucie Village, FL

$1,250,000 • 4 bed • 3 bath • 3,200 sq ft

Description

Riverfront retreat on the Indian River with private dock potential, lush mature landscaping, and panoramic water views from nearly every room. Situated in the quiet historic enclave of St. Lucie Village just north of Fort Pierce, this property captures sunrise over the river and a true Old Florida feel.

Spacious split floor plan, vaulted ceilings, and oversized sliders frame the water views. A screened lanai and large backyard offer space for entertaining, gardening, or simply watching dolphins and manatees pass by.

The Village at Bishop’s Gate

A planned community of townhomes and condominiums in a wooded suburban setting.

Eight building groups arranged around a central green, with mature pines, walking paths, and a community clubhouse. The Village at Bishop’s Gate was master-planned in the late 1990s by an award-winning landscape architect and still reads beautifully today.

Property types

  • Townhomes — 1,800 to 2,400 sqft
  • Garden condominiums — 950 to 1,400 sqft
  • Penthouse condominiums — 1,650 to 2,100 sqft

Amenities

  • Clubhouse with kitchen & great room
  • Outdoor pool & sundeck
  • Tennis & pickleball courts
  • Pet-friendly common spaces
  • Walking trails throughout

Recent activity

Twelve closings over the past twelve months, with average days on market under 30 and average sale-to-list ratio above asking.

HOA & fees

Single monthly assessment covers grounds, exterior maintenance, amenity access, and trash. Fee varies by floor plan and unit type.

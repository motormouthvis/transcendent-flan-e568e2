Port Royal

A premier waterfront community of estate-sized lots, deep-water boat docks, and resort-style amenities.

Quietly tucked behind a gated entrance, Port Royal offers some of the largest single-family-home lots in the city, many with direct Gulf access. The Port Royal Club anchors the community with tennis, beach access, and a full-service dining program.

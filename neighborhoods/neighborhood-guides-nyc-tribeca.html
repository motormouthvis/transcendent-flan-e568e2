Welcome to Tribeca

Cast-iron lofts, cobblestone streets, and a quiet that’s rare for Manhattan.

Homes for Sale in Tribeca Homes for Rent in Tribeca
What to expect

An air of discreet luxury layered on top of an old shipping-and-cheese district. Restaurants here are reservations-only and worth it.

The lifestyle

Family-friendly, surprisingly. Some of the best public schools in Manhattan. Stroller parking lines the windows of certain bakeries.

Unexpected appeal

Hudson River Park is at your doorstep. So is the ferry to New Jersey, if you ever want to leave.

The market

High price points and limited inventory. Lofts sell quickly when they hit. Townhouses are a generational hold.

Located in Manhattan, NYC

Boundaries: Roughly Canal St. to the north, Vesey to the south, Broadway to the east, the Hudson to the west.

Nearest Subways: Chambers St (1/2/3), Canal St (A/C/E), Franklin St (1).

Nearby neighborhoods

Battery Park City, Financial District, SoHo, West Village.

Arts District

Walk Score: 91 · Bike Score: 88 · Transit Score: 78

A converted-warehouse arts neighborhood with rooftop bars, gallery row, and an outdoor coffee culture year-round.

Once the city’s industrial backbone, this neighborhood has been reborn as a cultural anchor. The old produce-market buildings now house galleries, design studios, restaurants, and lofts whose floor-plates are too generous for most modern construction to replicate.

Lifestyle

Walk to the coffee shop. Walk to the gallery opening. Walk to dinner. The Arts District rewards people who like the city on foot.

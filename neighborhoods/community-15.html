About our community

Mockingbird Heights is a brand-new single-family-home community now selling in one of the fastest-growing corridors in the metroplex. Floor plans range from 1,950 to 3,400 square feet, with three to five bedrooms, and most homesites are oversized lots with mature trees preserved from the original ranch property.

Residents will enjoy a community pavilion, a future amenity pool, walking trails connecting to the regional park system, and a top-rated school district with the elementary located inside the community itself.

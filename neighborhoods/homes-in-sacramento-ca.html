Welcome

Discover one of the most coveted lifestyles in the capital region — tree-lined streets, walkable parks, a beloved zoo, and homes that have been carefully restored over generations. It’s a place where neighbors know each other and where weekend mornings start at the farmers market.

What to expect

A laid-back, family-oriented vibe with a strong sense of place. Bungalows, Tudors, and craftsman cottages line the streets. The neighborhood centers around its namesake park, which features rose gardens, a duck pond, and a historic amphitheater.

The lifestyle

Walk to the zoo on Saturday morning. Bike the trails to the river. Pick up sourdough at the corner bakery. The pace here is slower than downtown but everything is within a few minutes’ drive of the dome.

Nearby neighborhoods

Curtis Park, East Sacramento, Midtown, and Hollywood Park.

Boundaries

Roughly bounded by Sutterville Road to the south, Broadway to the north, the freeway to the east, and the river to the west.

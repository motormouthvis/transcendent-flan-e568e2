Melbourne, FL - Neighborhood Detail (6165 Isla St Area)

Located at 6165 Isla St in Melbourne, this property sits in a desirable established neighborhood characterized by spacious lots, lush tropical vegetation, and family-friendly streets. Melbourne's Space Coast location provides residents with quick access to downtown's restaurants and shops, the Indian River waterfront, pristine Atlantic beaches, and major employers including Harris Corporation and Northrop Grumman. The area boasts excellent public schools, numerous parks, community events, and a growing tech and aerospace economy, making it an ideal setting for both families and professionals seeking Florida's best lifestyle.

Why Buyers Choose Melbourne
  • Strong job market in tech, aerospace & defense
  • Excellent schools and family amenities
  • Close to beaches, river, and downtown
  • Growing real estate values with strong demand
  • Balanced suburban lifestyle with urban access

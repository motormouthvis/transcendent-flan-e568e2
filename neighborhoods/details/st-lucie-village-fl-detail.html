St. Lucie Village, FL - Neighborhood Detail

St. Lucie Village is a historic, waterfront enclave nestled along the Indian River in St. Lucie County. This charming neighborhood is known for its mature oak hammocks, stunning river views, and laid-back Old Florida character. Residents enjoy private dock access, excellent fishing opportunities, and a strong sense of community in one of the area's most desirable locations. The village offers a peaceful retreat with easy access to both the Atlantic beaches and the vibrant downtown Fort Pierce, combining natural beauty with convenient proximity to urban conveniences.

Why Buyers Choose St. Lucie Village
  • Direct Indian River waterfront access
  • Historic Old Florida charm with modern estates
  • Excellent fishing and boating
  • Quiet, established community with large lots
  • Short drive to beaches and Fort Pierce amenities

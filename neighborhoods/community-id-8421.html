Riverwood at Mill Creek

A growing waterfront community with model homes by three regional builders.

Riverwood occupies a former farmstead along a slow-moving tributary, with most homesites either on the water or one row back. The first phase is sold out; phases two and three are selling now, with closings scheduled through the end of next year.
